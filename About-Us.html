Our Mission

At Fauna Hospital, our mission is to provide dedicated care and rehabilitation to injured, sick, and orphaned wildlife, ensuring their well-being and eventual return to their natural habitats. We are committed to the preservation and conservation of our local fauna, striving to create a harmonious balance between human activity and the environment. With a team of experienced veterinarians, rehabilitators, and passionate volunteers, we work tirelessly to mend the wounds of our animal friends, offering them a second chance at life in the wild. Our mission is not just about healing creatures; it's about fostering a deeper understanding of the vital role that wildlife plays in our world and inspiring others to join us in safeguarding the delicate ecosystems that sustain us all.
